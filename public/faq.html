Everything You Need to Know About Selling Your Land

How quickly can I get an offer on my land?

We can typically provide a cash offer within 24-48 hours of receiving your property information. For larger or more complex properties, it may take slightly longer, but we pride ourselves on fast, fair offers.

Do you buy land in all 50 states?

Yes! We purchase land throughout the United States. Whether your property is in a major city or a rural area, we're interested in making you an offer.

What types of land do you purchase?

We buy all types of land: residential lots, commercial parcels, agricultural land, rural acreage, waterfront property, and more. If you own land, we're interested!

Are there any fees or commissions?

Absolutely not. We cover all closing costs, and there are no commissions or hidden fees. The offer we make is the amount you'll receive at closing.

What if I owe back taxes on my property?

No problem! We frequently purchase properties with back taxes owed. In many cases, we can pay off the back taxes as part of the purchase, so you don't have to worry about them.

How long does closing take?

We can close on your timeline, typically within 2-4 weeks. If you need to close faster, we can often accommodate that. If you need more time, that's fine too. We work on your schedule.

Do I need to survey my property before selling?

No, you don't need to get a new survey. We can work with existing surveys or legal descriptions. If a survey is needed, we'll handle that as part of our due diligence.

What documents do I need to provide?

At minimum, we need the property address or APN (Assessor's Parcel Number). If you have a deed, survey, or title report, that's helpful but not required. We'll guide you through any documentation needed.

Can I sell my land if I don't live nearby?

Absolutely! We handle many remote closings. The entire process can be done via mail, email, and electronic documents. You don't need to travel or be physically present at closing.

Is my offer obligation-free?

Yes! Our offers come with no obligation or pressure. You can review the offer, discuss it with family or advisors, and make the decision that's right for you. We won't hassle you.
